About

Built by JETs, for JETs

Hokuriku JETs is a community resource site for JET Programme participants living and working across Ishikawa and Toyama Prefecture. It collects the practical stuff — events, resources, people to contact, and how-to guides — in one place that's easy to update and easy to find things on.

Get in touch

Have something to add, fix, or ask about? Reach out at ishikawaresources@gmail.com.

For site admins

Editing events, resources, people, or guides requires signing in with a HokuSpot moderator/admin account — use the "Admin sign in" button in the top right of any page. There's no separate account system for this site.
